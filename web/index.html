A heatmap of aggregate bike trips throughout the city from a selected station, answering the question: "where do people bike from here?" All trips from 2013 (750K of them) supplied by Divvy. Each region roughly corresponds to the area within half a mile from the Divvy station (i.e. places you'd walk from/to).
